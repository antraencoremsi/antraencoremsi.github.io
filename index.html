About Us

Antra Encore -The official Music Society of Maharaja Surajmal Institute, is about passion, talent and pure music reverberating from all our collective soul. It is run by a group of Dedicated individuals who are passionate about music.
‘Antra’ is the division for Indian Music, While
‘Encore’ is the division for Western Music.
Our Aim is to provide a Platform for upcoming music lovers to showcase their talents and take our talents to new heights.
