About Us

Antra Encore -The official Music Society of Maharaja Surajmal Institute, is about passion, talent and pure music reverberating from all our collective soul. It is run by a group of Dedicated individuals who are passionate about music.
‘Antra’ is the division for Indian Music, While
‘Encore’ is the division for Western Music.
Our Aim is to provide a Platform for upcoming music lovers to showcase their talents and take our talents to new heights.

Re-Auditions are here!

If you are a Vocalist, Musician, Beatboxer, Composer, Producers, DJ, Writer or Simply Passionate about Music, then this is the place you should be.
Antra Encore -The Music Society of Maharaja Surajmal Institute.

Rules :-
  1. Applicants who appeared for Auditions last week are not allowed to enter in re-auditions, this only for people who missed the last week auditions.
  2. Time and Venue will be informed to the applicants on call.
  3. Carry your own instrument.
  4. Max stage time is 1:30 mins.
